Fake News Or Not?!

With fake news rampant, there are opportunities for those who can tell fact from fiction. You've just taken up a new role as a newspaper reviewer, and it's your responsibility to flag stories that may require more verification.

Letting fake stories through isn't acceptable, but management also don't want to commit resources to fact-checking stories that don't require it. What a conundrum! Can your gut tell the fake news headlines from those that sound too bizarre to be true...but yet are?

How To Play

  • Click the turn page button to start the game
  • Enter your name; this is only for display purposes and will not be used anywhere or stored
  • You will have 10 stories to identify whether they are fake or real
  • Read the headline and then click the fake or real button to submit your decision
  • A popup will tell you if you're right or wrong and provide more details about the story. Click "Next Story" to proceed
  • When you have reviewed 10 stories, your final score will pop up and you'll have a chance to share with friends or start again
