Fake News Or Not?!

By The Editors

16 January 2021

You have just taken up a new role as a newspaper reviewer, and it's your responsibility to flag stories that may require more fact-checking.

In an ideal world, you would have the full story available and time to fact-check. But management, in all its wisdom, does not want you to spend time reading stories, nor does it want to commit resources to fact-checking stories willy-nilly. What a conundrum!

With just the newspaper headlines to guide you, can your gut tell the fake news headlines from those that sound too bizarre to be true...but somehow are?
